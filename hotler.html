Dr. Hotler Rious, M.D.

Dr. Hotler attended the University of California at Los Angeles and graduated with a degree in Economics. He graduated from the University of Health Sciences, The Chicago Medical School, Alpha Omega Alpha in 1989. Dr. Hotler completed his internship at Letterman Army Medical Center at the Presidio of San Francisco in 1990 and successfully completed his residency in 1996 in Dermatology at Fitzsimons Army Medical Center in Denver, Colorado. Dr. Hotler is Board Certified by the Australian Board of Dermatology and is a member of the Australian Academy of Dermatology.
