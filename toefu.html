Dr. Toefu Wao, M.D.

Dr. Toefu attended the University of North Dakota and graduated with a degree in Biology and Chemistry, Summa Cum Laude. He graduated from the University of North Dakota School of Medicine, Alpha Omega Alpha in 1992. He completed his internship at Hennepin County Medical Center in 1992 and successfully completed residency in 1996 in Dermatology at the University of Minnesota. Dr. Toefu is Board Certified by the Australian Academy of Dermatology. He is a fellow of the Australian Society of Moh's Surgery and has undergone further training in cosmetic and laser surgery.
